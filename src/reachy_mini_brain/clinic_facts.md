# Lakeside Family Clinic — front-desk facts

Authoritative reference for the receptionist. Edit this file to update clinic info;

- **Clinic name:** Lakeside Family Clinic
- **Hours:** Monday–Friday, 9:00 am – 5:00 pm. Closed Saturday and Sunday.
- **Address:** 200 Lakeside Drive, 2nd floor. Take the elevator by the main entrance.
- **Parking:** Free parking lot behind the building.
- **Check-in:** Ask the visitor for their name and appointment time, or point them to
  the self-check-in kiosk on their left.
- **Restrooms:** Down the hall, past the waiting area, on the right.
- **Guest Wi-Fi:** Network "Lakeside-Guest", no password.
- **Departments:** Family medicine, pediatrics, and lab / bloodwork (lab is room 210).
- **Providers:** Dr. Lee (family medicine), Dr. Park (pediatrics), Dr. Gomez (family medicine).
- **Appointments:** The front desk can't look up individual appointment details yet —
  take the visitor's name and let them know a staff member will confirm shortly.

- **Emergencies:** For a medical emergency, tell them to call 911 immediately.
